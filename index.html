About Me

I’m constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities for growth, which is why I’m passionate about combining my interest in both business and technology. Currently studying Wirtschaftsinformatik at BFH Bern, I’m gaining the skills and knowledge needed to bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions. I believe that technology has the power to transform the way we do business, and I’m eager to be part of this exciting journey.

When I’m not studying or working on projects, I love to spend my time riding my Harley Davidson. The open road gives me a sense of freedom and adventure that I truly enjoy. Whether I’m hiking through scenic landscapes, exploring unfamiliar cities, skiing, or planning my next adventure, I’m always looking for new experiences that challenge me and help me grow.

I’m focused on continuous learning and personal development. My studies, hobbies, and future career goals are all aligned with my desire to embrace new opportunities, especially in the ever-evolving fields of business and technology.
